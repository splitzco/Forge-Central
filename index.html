How it works
Designed around
the officer.
01
One officer record
Every officer exists once in FORGE. Their training history, certifications, assignments, and wellness data all connect to a single source of truth.
02
Apps plug in
Each FORGE app connects to the central record. No duplicate data entry. No siloed systems. An officer completes training — every app knows instantly.
03
Agencies stay in control
Admins configure which apps are active, which features are enabled, and which issuing authorities govern compliance — all from one portal.
